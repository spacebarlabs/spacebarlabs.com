# Security at Space Bar Labs

Space Bar Labs is committed to ensuring the safety and security of our users and data. We welcome the contribution of external security researchers and appreciate responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in one of our products or platforms, please report it to us via email at:

**security@spacebarlabs.com**

Please include:

* A description of the vulnerability.
* Steps to reproduce the issue (POCs or screenshots are helpful).
* The specific URL or endpoint affected.

## Scope

**In Scope:**

* spacebarlabs.com
* cloudbreak.app
* sunya.plus
* Any applications we manage (e.g., freshrss.cloudbreak.app)

**Out of Scope:**

* Denial of Service (DoS) attacks.
* Social engineering or phishing of our employees.
* Physical attacks against our infrastructure.
* Third-party applications we do not manage.

## Our Promise (Safe Harbor)

If you act in good faith and follow this policy, Space Bar Labs:

1. Will **not** pursue legal action against you regarding your research.
2. Will work with you to understand and resolve the issue quickly.
3. Will acknowledge your report and keep you updated on our progress.

## Recognition

We are happy to publicly acknowledge researchers who submit valid security reports in our release notes or Hall of Fame, if desired.
